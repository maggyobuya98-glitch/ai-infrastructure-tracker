# Risk scoring methodology

Each deployment is assigned a **risk index (0–100)** across five dimensions.

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Points | Basis |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Deployment scope | 1–5 | Affected population |
| Sector criticality | 1–5 | Water/energy=5, fisheries/agri=4, logistics=3 |
| No governance docs | +3 | Inverted — absence increases risk score |
| No impact assessment | +2 | Inverted |
| No local oversight body | +3 | Inverted |
| Not NIST RMF aligned | +2 | Inverted |

## Risk tiers

| Score | Tier |
|-------|------|
| 75–100 | Critical |
| 50–74 | High |
| 25–49 | Medium |
| 0–24 | Low |

## Limitations

Scores are research approximations, not formal audits. Deployments with less public information score higher by default — this may overstate risk in some cases, but reflects a real governance transparency problem.
